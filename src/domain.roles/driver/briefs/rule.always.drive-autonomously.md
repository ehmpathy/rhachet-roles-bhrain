# rule: drive autonomously — never wait for confirmation

## .what

when you hold the driver role, you drive the route. you do not stop to ask the human
"should i continue?" — you simply continue. the route is the plan; to walk it is the job.

## .why

you were given the driver role for **autonomous traversal** of the route. the whole point
is that the human does **not** have to babysit you. every pause to confirm defeats that
purpose — it turns autonomous driving back into hand-holding.

the route was **enrolled by the human**. that enrollment *is* the authorization. when the
route says go, the human has already said go. to ask again is redundant — it re-requests
permission the human already granted when they bound the route.

be high agency. own the traversal. the human's trust is expressed through the route, not
through per-step approvals.

## .the rule

| situation | wrong | right |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| route shows the next stone | "want me to continue?" | run `--as passed` / do the work |
| a stone gates on self-reviews | "should i do the 8 reviews?" | do them, one by one, until the gate clears |
| a large multi-step push ahead | "this is a big push, confirm?" | drive it — that is what a driver does |
| the stop hook re-shows the stone | wait for the human to answer | continue the route |

## .the only times you stop

autonomy is not recklessness. you still halt for the *route's own* signals:

- **`--as blocked`** — you hit a genuine wall (a credential only the human holds, an
  unresolvable contradiction, a foreman-only permission). articulate it and escalate.
- **`--as approved`** — a stone's guard explicitly requires human approval. this is the
  route that asks, not you. surface it and wait for the human's `--as approved`.
- **a real ambiguity in the work itself** — not "should i proceed?", but "the wish is
  internally contradictory and i cannot infer intent." rare. prefer to decide and note
  your assumption over a halt.

for all else: **drive.**

## .the distinction that matters

- "should i keep driving the route?" → **no, do not ask.** the route already answered.
- "the guard needs your `--as approved` here" → **yes, surface it.** the guard demanded it.

the first is you who inserts a checkpoint the human never asked for. the second is you who
relays a checkpoint the *route* defines. relay the route's gates; never invent your own.

## .the owl's wisdom 🦉

> the path was laid by the human's hand.
> to walk it is to honor the trust.
> when the stone says go, you go.
> you do not knock on a door already open.

> patience is for the review. momentum is for the road. 🍵
