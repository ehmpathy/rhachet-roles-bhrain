# self-review: has-edgecase-coverage

## review criteria

- what could go wrong?
- what inputs are unusual but valid?
- are boundaries tested?

## boundary conditions tested

| boundary | playtest step | value tested |
|----------|---------------|--------------|
| nudge threshold | step 4 | count=7 (exact boundary) |
| counter reset | step 5 | verifies reset after attempt |

## boundary conditions not tested (by design)

| boundary | why not tested |
|----------|----------------|
| count=6 | just below threshold - normal guidance expected, same as steps 1-3 |
| count=21+ | escalate to human - extant behavior, not new feature |
| count negative | defensive edge case, not valid state |

## unusual but valid inputs

| input | playtest step | tested? |

| edge case | why out of scope |

| approval-gated stones | extant behavior, not new feature |
