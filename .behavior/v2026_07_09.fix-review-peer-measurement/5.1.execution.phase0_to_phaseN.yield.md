progress log for the phased execution of the blueprint
(`3.3.1.blueprint.product.yield.md`) against the roadmap
(`4.1.roadmap.yield.md`).

## summary

add a fallback cascade to the guard's per-review measurement: deterministic regex
first, then a cheap sub-brain (`fireworks/deepseek/v4-flash`) when an exit-0 review
states its verdict in prose. a review with no readable verdict still fails loud as a
malfunction. when the fallback fires, the guard shows a `tallied by reviewer@$brain`
marker. the return shape is a discriminated union that makes the failhide a compile error.
