dream = require driver response before peer review retry

---

we want drivers to respond to each review before another attempt can be issued

this prevents budget exhaustion when the driver hasn't even addressed the original review

---

currently:
- peer review runs, finds blockers
- driver re-runs route.drive
- peer review runs again, consumes budget
- repeat until exhausted
- driver never addressed the blockers

desired:
- peer review runs, finds blockers
- driver must acknowledge/respond before next attempt
- only after response can budget be consumed again

---

possible mechanisms:
- require artifact hash change between attempts (driver made changes)
- require explicit "i addressed this" signal
- track "last responded hash" vs "last reviewed hash"

---

this ensures budget is spent on meaningful iterations, not wasted on unchanged artifacts
