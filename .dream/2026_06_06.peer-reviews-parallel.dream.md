dream = run peer reviews in parallel

## .what

run multiple peer reviewers concurrently instead of sequentially

## .why

- faster guard evaluation when multiple reviewers are configured
- reviewers at the same level are independent, no reason to wait
- reduces total wall-clock time for multi-reviewer guards

## .how

- parallelize reviewers within the same level
- maintain level order (level 1 must complete before level 2 starts)
- aggregate results after all reviewers in a level complete
