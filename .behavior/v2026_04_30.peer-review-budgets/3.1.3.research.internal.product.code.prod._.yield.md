## cache semantics

**current behavior**:
- reviews cached by hash (artifact content)
- only `exitClass === 'passed'` cached
- constraints (blockers found) NOT cached — re-run on next attempt

**budget interaction**:
- cached reviews = no budget consumption (already approved)
- fresh reviews = budget consumption on successful exit (passed or constraint)
- malfunctions = no budget consumption (system error)
