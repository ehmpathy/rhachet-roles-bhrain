# rule.always.externalize.lessons.into_briefs

## .what

when you learn, write it down. externalize lessons into briefs before context fades.

## .when

externalize when:
- a concept was a surprise
- a concept was hard to discover
- you had to ask or research to understand
- you wish someone had told you sooner

## .why

- context fades — what's obvious now will be forgotten tomorrow
- lessons unwritten are lessons lost
- briefs compound — each one makes the next traveler faster
- the cost of capture is low; the cost of rediscovery is high

## .how

1. notice the lesson (surprise, friction, insight)
2. pause — don't wait until later
3. write a brief in `.agent/repo=.this/role=*/briefs/`
4. keep it short — future you will thank present you

## .the test

ask: "would i want to know this if i were new to this codebase?"

yes → write the brief
no → move on

## .mantra

> write it down before it fades 📜

## .enforcement

lesson learned but not externalized = violation of learner purpose
