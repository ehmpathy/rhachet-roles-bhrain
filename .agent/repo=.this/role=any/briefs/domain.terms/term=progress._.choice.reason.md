# domain.term.choice.reason: progress

## .etymology
**progress** names forward motion toward a goal — here, the learner's motion through its
domain-term lessons. chosen over `status` (a state snapshot, not a record of what advanced),
`log` (an append-only stream, whereas progress is a rewritten single record), and `journal`
(diary-flavored, personal rather than a shared sweep record).

## .disputes
none. no contract has drifted to a synonym of `progress`.

## .evidence
- discovery: the wish named it — "in the progress.md, what we want to see is a declaration of
  whether it was done and what the result was and why". the noun `progress` composes the two
  declared operations that read the sentinel (`getSweepProgress`, `isProgressArticulated`)
- invariants: the progress record is the ONE hard requirement of a sweep — every other judgment
  (which terms, which synonyms) is the learner's, but the progress articulation is owed each round
