also — the blocked experience is wrong today.

when a stone is blocked and someone re-arrives it, the line still reads "blocked". but a `--as blocked` was a driver escalation — the moment anyone sets the stone to any new status, that escalation is stale. it must clear.

and if the re-arrival fails again, the onStop hook should keep the route in motion — drive it forward — not sit halted. the route should halt its self-drive only when it genuinely cannot proceed alone — a real wall (✋ stophand) or a malfunction (💥 collision). it should show the 👋 wave when it only awaits a human to approve.

to make that clean, pull **exhausted** out into its own status, separate from blocked — an exhausted peer review means "a human must approve or extend", a 👋 case, not a stuck ✋. keep **approval** as its own clear blocker. that leaves **blocked** to mean only the hard wall.

the statusline emoji and the onStop push/halt must be one truth: the halt glyphs (👋 / ✋ / 💥) appear exactly when the route will not drive itself, 🌾/🔍 when it will.
