## the key design correction (caught by #288's tests)

"active level" (what overrule targets) is a **stricter** notion than `isReviewPeerLevelTerminal`
(which gates tier escalation). #288 makes malfunction/constraint terminal for *escalation*,
but those verdicts still **block passage** — so they are overrule-able. the active level is
the lowest level not yet clear for passage, where a level is clear only when every reviewer
at it is `approved` (or the level is overruled). this fix made #288's cases 13/14 pass under
the level-scoped model.
