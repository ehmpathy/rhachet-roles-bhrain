## matrix.3: stone state before rewind

| ind: stone state | dep: guard artifacts deleted | dep: passage entry appended | dep: outcome |
|------------------|------------------------------|----------------------------|--------------|
| never validated  | n/a (none exist)             | yes                        | no-op, safe  |
| validated        | yes                          | yes                        | cleared      |
| already rewound  | n/a (already cleared)        | yes                        | idempotent   |

## matrix.4: cascade scope

| ind: stone position | dep: guard artifacts deleted | dep: passage entry appended |
|---------------------|------------------------------|----------------------------|

| downstream (after)  | yes                          | yes                        |
| upstream (before)   | no                           | no                         |

## matrix.5: guard artifact types

|---------------------------------------|------------------------|
| *.guard.review.*                      | yes                    |

| stone content (the artifact itself)   | NO                     |

## matrix.6: approval invalidation

| ind: approval time | ind: rewind time | ind: rewind scope | dep: approval valid |
|--------------------|------------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| T1                 | T2 > T1          | same stone        | invalid             |
| T1                 | T2 > T1          | earlier stone     | invalid             |
| T1                 | T2 > T1          | later stone       | valid               |
| T1                 | T2 < T1          | any               | valid               |

key insight: approvals are invalidated by log order, not deleted. a rewind entry that appears AFTER an approval (for same or earlier stone) makes that approval stale.
