**rule**: .agent/repo=bhuild/role=behaver/briefs/practices/behavior.execution/architect/rule.forbid.friction-hazards.md

**locations**:
- src/domain.operations/review/stepReview.integration.test.ts

The primary user-facing output (review.formatted) is only checked with .toLowerCase().includes('blocker') or length > 0. No snapshots of the actual output for positive/negative/edge paths. This violates output consistency and friction test coverage: unsnapped outputs drift without notice, callers cannot vibecheck in PRs, regressions ship silently.             'blocker',

