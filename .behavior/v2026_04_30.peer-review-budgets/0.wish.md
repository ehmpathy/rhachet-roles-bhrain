wish =

we want to support budgets, configurable per peerer

specifically, assume that we upgrade to declare a slug and hashbar and `run` arg for peer reviews already, symmetric to how the self reviews have their structure in declaration (an open pr is inflight on this already)

lets extend that structure and add a 

- budget: $rounds


--

where 

- budget: $rounds  = the number of rounds we have budgeted for this review to get invoked

---

if that review has said "all good" already, then its already cached and budget wont be used, so thats not a factor


but if the artifact hash changes or there were blockers on prior review, then each subsequent invocation (ie. any time we actually call the review run command), consumes one budget token if it succeeds (not on malfunction, only on successful review completion, regardless of blockers or not - it should successfully exit)


so, after it consumes N tokens of budget, it stops and has a clear stdout that says 'exhausted'

that reviewer is exhausted.

---

then, after the rest of the reviewers are all in a terminal state (exhausted | approved)

if any of the reviewers were exhausted, the route must halt and ask the human to approve by hand

it should tell them that one or more of the reviewrs were exhausted

and ask for confirmation on whether they'd like to increase the budget (which they can run via a `rhx route.guard.budget --for review --peer $slug --add $rounds`) OR whether they'd like to approve it as is

---

that basically triggers an implicit --approved? judge, when any of them were exhausted

on top of one that may already exist

wit hthe caveat that we now must tell them this is the reason we asked them to approve

(and once they approve for either, they approve for both. its still juts one approval flag. it just is presented differently if its triggered first by exhaustion)

---

also, we need to support tiers here too

i.e., we want to run the tiers of smol brains before we start the tiers of big brains

that way, we dont waste the big brain costs on issues that smol brains can catch

---

e.g.,


in addition to budget, add level: $number 

---

so, 

- slug: cheapo
- budget: 10
- level: 1


- slug: cheap2
- budget: 10
- level: 1

- slug: primo
- budget: 3
- level: 2
