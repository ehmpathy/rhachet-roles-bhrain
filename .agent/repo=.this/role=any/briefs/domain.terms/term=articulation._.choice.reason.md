# domain.term.choice.reason: articulation

## .etymology
to **articulate** is to give clear, jointed expression to a thought — from latin *articulus*,
a joint. the word carries exactly the demand here: not a mark that an act occurred, but a
spelled-out account with real substance. chosen over `note` (too slight — a note can be a word),
`writeup` (informal), and `explanation` (implies you justify to someone, whereas articulation is
the learner's own crystallized account).

## .disputes
none. no contract has drifted to a synonym of `articulation`.

## .evidence
- discovery: the vision named the demand — "the ONE hard requirement is progress.md: the
  learner's articulation of their domain.term lesson progress". the adj `articulated` composes
  the declared guard `isProgressArticulated`
- invariants: articulation is content-shaped, not existence-shaped — the `isProgressArticulated`
  guard rejects a bare touch (an empty or whitespace-only sentinel), so a mark cannot pass as an
  account. THIS is the guard that a reasoned deferral wrongly satisfied — it checks that the
  content is real, not that a term was captured (a known limit, logged in progress.md)
